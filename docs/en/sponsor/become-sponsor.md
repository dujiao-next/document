# Become a Sponsor

> Updated: 2026-02-12

Thank you for supporting the Dujiao-Next open-source project ❤️

Sponsorship funds are used for:

- Continuous maintenance and bug fixes
- New feature development and documentation improvements
- Community support and release operations

## 1. Sponsor Benefits

As a sponsor, you can get the following exposure and benefits:

- Brand placement on the documentation homepage (by sponsor tier)
- Brand placement in the right sidebar ad area (by sponsor tier)
- Display of your brand name, logo, description, and link in sponsor modules
- Priority response from maintainers for sponsor feedback and communication

## 2. Sponsorship Tiers

> The following are standard tiers. Custom plans are available on request.

| Tier | Monthly Fee | Placement Rights | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Platinum Sponsor | 300 USD / month | Homepage sponsor block + right sidebar ad | Best exposure, recommended for brand partnerships |
| Gold Sponsor | 200 USD / month | Right sidebar ad | Stable visibility for long-term promotion |
| Silver Sponsor | 100 USD / month | Sponsor directory page | Entry-level brand exposure |

### Asset Recommendations

- Recommended image ratio: 16:9 or equivalent logo-friendly ratio
- Recommended copy: title + one short description
- Recommended link target: official website / campaign page / product page
- If no image is provided, text-only rendering (title + description) is supported
